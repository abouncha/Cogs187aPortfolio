ABOUT ME


My name is Annie Bounchaleun and I am a Designer and Developer currently attending University of California, San Diego. I love Photography, Traveling, Music, and Art. Such interests can be seen influencing my works.
I enjoy photographing sceneries or candids of close family and friends. You can find some of my photographs by scrolling down! I enjoy comfort, but am still open to trying new things, and exploring new cultures and places. I love spicy food and currently on the hunt to try as much spicy foods as I can get my hands on.
